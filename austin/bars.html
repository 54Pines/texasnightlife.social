Don't get lost in Austin

HandleBar



121 E. 5th Street

Get off 6th Street for a bit. This spot celebrates the great mustache as well as an amazing rooftop deck for Austin nights. This is also the spot to be for Sunday Funday. While you're there, pick up on of their famous calendars. Definitely a place to get that Austin local feel.

Lustre Pearl



94 Rainey Street

Lustre Pearl is located on Austin's Rainey Steet. Whether it be a perfect Sunday Funday or a weekend night, check out this remodled old house. This bar is mostly open aired with the second floor deck, and the large yard around the house. Bring your dog, and buy one of the best selling hats in Austin.

Dirty Bills



511 Rio Grande Street

This could be the best dive bar in all of Austin. Located on West 6th, this small, dark bar has great bartenders, great music, and the best movies playing on the TVs. While you are there, make sure you get a picture with the world famous gnome.

Dizzy Rooster



306 E. 6th Street

Located in the heart of 6th street. This bar always has a huge range of people of all ages. Live music, girls dacing on the bar, this dive bar is a must stop when on Dirty Six. If you're looking for a good happy hour, this is a hot spot too.

Dogwood



715 W. 6th Street

The outdoor patio bar is the key aspect to this place. There is a large stage for live music, and if you are looking to be outside for happy hour or a perfect Texas night, this is the spot on West 6th. The crowd is a little more upscale with mostly young professionals. Check out their food menu too for great eats.

Green Light Social



720 W. 6th Street

This is the newest hot spot on West 6th. The entire bar is upstairs on a rooftop overlooking downtown. You can even use the free binoculars to check out some other spots. Get there early to avoid the line on weekends! Don't forget to get an adult Caprise Sun while you are there. Perfect stop for Sunday Funday too.

Bring out your big-kid-self at Kung Fu Saloon. They have all the classic video games as well as giant jenga, giant connect four, skeeball and a giant gong they might let you ring if you ask nicely.

The Market



319 Colorado Street

The Market and Taproom, located in the Warehouse District, have an old time vintage feel. The pallets and old barrels around the bar really give it a unique vibe. Try one of their handmade craft cocktails while on the rooftop patio enjoying the best view in downtown Austin.

Blind Pig Pub



317 E. 6th Street

You can't come to Austin without checking out the Blind Pig Pub. This is one of Austin's largest bars with a downstairs, back deck, and huge rooftop to grab a beer or cocktail. There is acoustic live music downstairs each night as well as full bands upstairs each night.

Little Woodrows



520 W. 6th Street

Located on West 6th, this bar is the perfect Austin style bar. The bar is opened aired, has plenty of room to kick back and relax on a large picnic bench, or sit at the bar with the pretties girls who serve you any of the 70 beer choices. Did we mention this is where the Seahawk Club meets during football season?

If your mom ask, you're at The Library. Fortunately, this library isn't full of books but beautiful bartenders and cold drinks. Located in the heart of 6th street, this is a classic Dirty Six bar with large tv's, pool tables upstairs, and a VIP balcony. If it's a hot Austin day, grab a frozen drink here or one off their specialty menu.

Thirsty Nickel



325 E. 6th Street

The Thirsty Nickel is THE Chicago Bar here in Austin Texas. Want to catch the Bears, Blackhawks or World Champions Cubs, this is the place for you. Newly remodeled, you'll love the fun bartenders, all the TV's for games and the classic Sixth Street bar look..

Hangar Lounge



318 Colorado Street

Hangar Lounge is located in the Warehouse District of Downtown Austin. This bar truly does feel like you're in a Hangar. The 3 stories offers you everything you could need from a classy surrounding, to a rooftop overlooking the city. The first floor is a lounge with bar and nice seating. The second floor is a perfect spot for bottle service or to have more of a private group feeling, and the rooftop offers a 2nd bar and plenty of seating. Great bar for happy hour or to just relax with your friends.
